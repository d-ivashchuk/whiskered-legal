# Privacy Policy

**Whiskered — Mewgenics Companion**
Last updated: April 24, 2026

## What the app does

Whiskered is a companion app for the game Mewgenics. It lets you browse game data (items, classes, abilities, sets, status effects) and scan in-game items using your device's camera.

## Data we collect

### Analytics (optional)

We use [PostHog](https://posthog.com) to collect anonymous usage data — things like which screens you visit and which features you use. This helps us understand how people use the app so we can improve it.

- Analytics are **on by default** but you can **turn them off** any time in Settings.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.
- We do not sell your data to anyone.

### Crash reports

We use [Sentry](https://sentry.io) to collect crash reports and error data. This includes device type, OS version, and the error that occurred. This helps us find and fix bugs.

### Camera

The app uses your camera to scan and identify game items. All image processing happens **entirely on your device** using an on-device machine learning model (CoreML). No photos or camera data are ever uploaded to any server.

### Photo library

When you choose to pick an image from your photo library for scanning, the image is processed **on-device only**. We do not upload, store, or share your photos.

## Data we do NOT collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No contacts or address book data
- No photos or camera data sent to servers
- No tracking across apps or websites

## In-app purchases

Purchases are handled through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not process or store any payment information. All billing is managed by Apple.

## Children's privacy

Whiskered is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
